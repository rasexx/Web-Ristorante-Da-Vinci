The Ristorante Da Vinci project aims to create an online presence for a restaurant inspired by the genius of Leonardo da Vinci and the exquisiteness of Italian cuisine. This website offers a sophisticated and elegant experience, reflecting Italian culture and a passion for gastronomy, specially designed to attract food lovers in Cartagena, Colombia.

## 🚩 Key Features
- **Menu Presentation:** A detailed gallery of our dishes and drinks.
- **Online Reservations:** Easy and quick system for reserving tables.
- **"Why Us?" Section:** Highlighting the essence and passion of the restaurant.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for all platforms, from mobile to desktop.
